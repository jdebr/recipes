# Parmesan Crusted <meat>

## Ingredients
- Butter **1/4 cup**
- Panko **2 Tbsp**
- Parmesan **1/4 cup**
- Granulated Garlic **2 tsp**
- Salt, Pepper, Parsley **optional**

## Instructions
1. Mash it all up and throw it in the fridge
2. Make steak
3. Pile it on top
4. Throw it under the broiler until it's a little brown
